Welcome to Skull 'O Rama!

The rules are simple: to start, a random score number, or target score, will be generated at the beginning of the game and shown to you. You are also given a score (starting at 0) based on points you earn while playing.

The 4 skulls buttons have random point values assigned to them similar to the target score, but with a catch: You aren't told what the values are until you have already gotten points from clicking the buttons!

If you manage to figure things out and get your score to equal the target score, you win! But if you go over the target score by so little as a single point, you lose. Have fun!
